Video Description

This video demonstrates coordinated response and aftercare during a fire emergency in a kitchen. It shows how to organize suppression and evacuation, communicate with emergency services, check for remaining hazards, and provide support for affected persons.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand how to coordinate roles (evacuation, suppression, reporting) during an emergency.
  • Learn how to safely communicate with responders and document the incident.
  • Perform aftercare steps: checking for smouldering spots, ensuring safety before re-entering, and emotional support.
