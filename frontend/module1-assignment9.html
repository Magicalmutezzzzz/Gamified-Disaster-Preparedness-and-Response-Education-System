Video Description

This detailed kitchen-safety lesson covers risk-aware cooking practices, correct handling of hot oil and grease, how to position cookware and handles to prevent knock-overs, safe use of electrical appliances in the kitchen, and step-by-step guidance for dealing with common cooking fires safely.

Learning Objectives

  • Recognize common kitchen hazards and eliminate them before cooking.
  • Apply immediate safe actions for small grease and appliance fires.
  • Practice layout and habits that reduce accident risk (handle positioning, pan size, appliance checks).
