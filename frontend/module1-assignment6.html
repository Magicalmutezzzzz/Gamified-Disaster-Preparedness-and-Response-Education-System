Video Description

A practical session on preventing household fires: safe cooking practices, electrical safety (avoiding overloaded sockets), safe storage of flammables, and daily checks to minimize fire risk. The video covers simple habits that greatly reduce the chance of an incident.

Learning Objectives

  • Identify common causes of household fires and how to prevent them.
  • Adopt everyday safe habits around cooking and electricity.
  • Prepare a basic emergency kit and ensure alarms are functional.
