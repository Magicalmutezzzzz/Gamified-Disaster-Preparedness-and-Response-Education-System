Video Description

This lesson demonstrates advanced on-site safety techniques applicable to small-to-medium fires: assessing risk quickly, choosing the correct suppression approach, safe use of tools (blankets, extinguishers), team coordination, and basic immediate aftercare. It emphasizes safety-first decision making and when to hand off to professionals.

Learning Objectives

  • Assess whether a fire is safe to attempt extinguishing.
  • Select an appropriate suppression tool and apply it with correct technique.
  • Coordinate with others and perform basic aftercare and documentation.
