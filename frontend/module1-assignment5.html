Video Description

This lesson compares extinguishing methods and shows demonstrations for several small-fire scenarios — grease, electrical and paper. It explains extinguisher classes, the PASS technique in action, and safe approach distances while putting out different fire types.

Learning Objectives

  • Match extinguisher types to common household fires.
  • Apply PASS correctly for effective extinguishing.
  • Know safe distances and when to stop and evacuate.
