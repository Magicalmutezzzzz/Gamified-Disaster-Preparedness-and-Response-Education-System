Video Description

This advanced session walks through complex kitchen emergency responses: multi-pan incidents, handling flare-ups during deep frying, joint coordination (two-person tactics), and when to withdraw and call firefighters. The presenter demonstrates escalation recognition, safe appliance shutdown under stress, and post-incident checks.

Learning Objectives

  • Recognize escalation signs that require professional intervention.
  • Coordinate with another person to control small multi-source incidents safely.
  • Perform safe shutdown and post-incident inspection procedures.
