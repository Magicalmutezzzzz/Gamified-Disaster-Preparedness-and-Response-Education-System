Video Description

This short demonstration focuses on rapid containment of small household fires: shutting off fuel/electric sources safely, smothering small pan fires using lids or fire blankets, and coordinating with others to maintain safe zones. Emphasis is on rapid, calm action and preserving escape routes.

Learning Objectives

  • Recognize fires that can be contained vs those requiring evacuation.
  • Perform safe shutdown of appliances or gas supply if possible.
  • Use smothering methods correctly and maintain escape routes.
